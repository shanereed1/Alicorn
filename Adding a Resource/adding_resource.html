Adding a Resource

Required*

When choosing a topic, place your resource in a topic that best fits the theme of what you're resource.
Seperate tags with commas and keep tags short, simple, and relative to your resource.
When writing a summary, give a brief overview of what the resource is about how it helped you.

Notifications:

You'll receive notifications through your email on any disscussion or upvotes taking place on the resource you're posting.
